I would like to join an archery club?


If you are a student or staff member of ucd you can join the ucd polar bears.
There is a fee of €20 to join. If you are not eligible to join our club you can find other clubs here which will be more suited to you

Is there a beginner course?


Yes, we have a beginners course.
It starts in September and each week covers a different topic, eg. anchor, release. This course will be run in the first semester only.
It will cover recurve but if you would like to learn barebow we have members which can train you.
At the end of the beginners course we hold a beginners competition, with a prize for the winners.
If you join late don't worry the coaches will teach you, you just need to tell them where you are in your training, eg. complete beginner/stance/anchor and the coach can work from there

How much is it to join the club?


We have a joining fee of €20 this helps cover insurance and equipment costs.

Are there competitions that the club takes part in?


Yes, there's an Inter-Varsities League which members take part in.
This is run between colleges in Ireland, you can find out more from the ISAA website including current and past IV results
