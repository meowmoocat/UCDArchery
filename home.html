About us

The facilities and training offered by the Archery Club are second to none. All equipment is supplied and senior members train beginners initially. Extra coaching with an official IAAA coach is also available to team members. Membership for beginners will now close after week three in semester two, this is to allow team members to focus on upcoming competitions. Whether you want to fill a trophy cabinet or just try something new, the Archery Club has a level to suit you.
